The Challenge

Four AI agents are processing patient data without a unified HIPAA policy — and the platform that solves this is sitting dormant

CareStack went live on March 1 — a major infrastructure win. But the systems around it (Salesforce, DoctorLogic, Swell, VoiceStack, OpenClaw) are not connected to it. Every practice acquisition compounds this gap: manual data re-entry across disconnected systems, AI agents operating without PII controls, and no cross-system orchestration. Meanwhile, MuleSoft — purchased to solve exactly this — has never been activated: 0% utilization on 5 of 7 core metrics. The first integration built on this platform pays for the entire 6-month investment.

The Risk You're Carrying Today

Four AI agents are processing PHI without unified PII masking, audit trails, or HIPAA-compliant governance

VoiceStack, Agentforce, OpenClaw, and DoctorLogic AI are each handling patient data independently — four separate audit trails, four separate exposure surfaces, zero unified policy. A single unmasked PII incident in any of these agents creates regulatory, financial, and reputational risk that dwarfs the cost of the platform. Flex Gateway solves this at the infrastructure layer: PII masking, audit logging, and access control applied uniformly across all four agents.

Beyond risk mitigation, Agent Fabric enables new capabilities: a Recruitment Concierge that briefs recruiters before they answer the phone, after-hours booking that creates CareStack appointments autonomously, and operational intelligence that surfaces patterns in real time.

Agent Fabric capabilities represent future state — enabled by the platform but not included in the 6-month proof-of-value scope.

Cost of the Current Path

The integration foundation is cheaper to build now than at 200 offices

Specialized Dental Partners — same founding year (2018), same PE model, now 3.6x larger at 275+ offices — hit the integration wall with 12 fragmented PMS systems and no unified data layer. Their CEO publicly acknowledged in January 2026: "We're going to make mistakes" on technology. Reactive band-aids (Pearly for A/R, Coupa for spend) cost more and solve less than a proactive integration layer. HighFive is at the exact inflection point where the foundation is cheaper to build at 62 offices than at 200.

A 6-month structured proof-of-value that activates the MuleSoft platform already owned, builds the CareStack integration bridge, governs AI agents under Flex Gateway, and produces documented ROI for Norwest's board package. 1. Recruitment Concierge

VoiceStack call inbound → Agent Fabric routes to Salesforce lookup → AI generates candidate briefing with practice history, compensation benchmarks, and open requisitions → Briefing delivered to recruiter before they answer the phone.

2. After-Hours Patient Booking

VoiceStack AI handles after-hours patient calls → Agent Fabric governs the interaction under HIPAA → CareStack appointment created autonomously → Patient confirmation sent via Swell → Zero human touch.

3. Operational Intelligence

CareStack production data + Salesforce pipeline + VoiceStack call patterns → trigger-based alerts: cancellation spikes at specific offices, no-show pattern detection, capacity optimization recommendations surfaced in real time.

4. Intelligent Document Processing

CV/license/DEA extraction for doctor onboarding via MuleSoft IDP. Documents uploaded → data extracted → validated → routed to CareStack credentialing + Salesforce pipeline + Paylocity HR. Eliminates manual re-entry across 3 systems.

5. Patient Golden Record (Informatica IDMC)

MDM across all CareStack data + referring provider networks. Probabilistic matching, survivorship rules, merge/unmerge. Phase 2 play when HighFive crosses 100+ offices and data divergence becomes the growth ceiling.

Compliance and HIPAA governance are byproducts of a well-architected platform — not the primary value proposition. The value is what your team can BUILD on this foundation.
